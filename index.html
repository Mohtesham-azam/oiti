Best Technical Institute in Kolkata

Overseas Industrial Technical Institute is a dedicated Technical Training and Testing Center for different crafts and trades. It is unique in its structure, mission and effective implementation of its schemes to train and succeed in placement of its trainees, especially overseas in lucrative assignments. Overseas ITI has a perpetual and continuous system for the assessment of the critical manpower requirement of industries in India and overseas. Training courses are designed and modified accordingly. Campus Tests and Interviews are conducted almost every month for selection and overseas placement of Trainees. We, Overseas Industrial Technical Institute are engaged in offering Training on NEBOSH IGC , NEBOSH HSW, Industrial Safety Management, Instrument Engineer, Supervisor, QA/QC Inspector, Foreman, Instrument Technician , Electrician, HVAC Technician and Engineering, AutoCAD Training,Plant view 3D Models, PLC, SCADA, DCS System,etc. We have years of experience and knowledge that enable us to offer Training Services to our clients. Our consultants are continuously trained and they concentrate on analyzing our clients’ requirements and preferences. A lot of stress is given to the ongoing training and development of our consultants and it is ensured that each of our consultant’sperformance is evaluated. We employ an effective strategy that comprises evaluation of a combination of organizational initiatives and individual goals. Employees are encouraged to identify training programs that increase their value in ever changing marketplace.
